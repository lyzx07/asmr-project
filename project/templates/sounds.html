Sounds

Different sounds have the ability to effect our mood, or even trigger a memory. Pleasant sounds, or sounds we percieve as calming or pleasing, can relax us. While negative or harsh sounds can increase anxiety, stress, and have a negative effect on our mood. What is pleasant to some may not be to all who hear it, so this is quite subjective.

Musical Frisson

The phenomenon of chills or goosebumps that come from a piece of music (or from any other aesthetic experience) is called frisson, and it's been one of the big mysteries of human nature since it was first described.
